Financial Analysis Through Ratios

Financial analysis through ratios is a systematic approach to evaluating the financial health and performance of an organization. This method involves the use of various financial ratios derived from the company’s financial statements, primarily the balance sheet, income statement, and cash flow statement. Ratios provide insights into different aspects of a business’s operations, including profitability, liquidity, efficiency, and solvency.
